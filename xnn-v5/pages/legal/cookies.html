This Cookie Policy explains how Xana National News ("XNN", "we", "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website and services. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help the site remember your preferences, analyze usage, and provide personalized content. Cookies may be "persistent" (saved between sessions) or "session" (deleted when you close your browser).

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the operation of the site (e.g., maintaining your language preference or keeping you logged in). These cannot be disabled.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous information about how visitors use the site (pages visited, errors). We use this data to improve functionality.
  • Functionality Cookies: Enable enhanced features, such as remembering your username or video player settings.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver ads relevant to you and your interests. They may also be used to limit the number of times you see an ad.

Your Choices

You can control or delete cookies in several ways:

  • Adjust your browser settings to refuse all or certain cookies; instructions are available via the browser's "Help" menu.
  • Use our cookie consent banner to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can revisit your choices at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the site footer.
  • Clear cookies stored on your device; note that this may remove saved preferences and require you to re-login.
