GDPR Compliance

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Xana National News is committed to complying with the European Unionâ€™s General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR"). This section outlines the rights of data subjects in the EU/EEA and the measures we have implemented to protect personal data.

Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal data only when we have a lawful basis, such as:

  • Consent (e.g. newsletter subscriptions, cookies).
  • Contractual necessity (e.g. user accounts, subscription services).
  • Legal obligation (e.g. responding to law enforcement requests).
  • Legitimate interests (e.g. improving site performance, fraud prevention).

Data Subject Rights

Individuals located in the EU/EEA have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Have inaccurate or incomplete data corrected.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of data where there is no legitimate reason to retain it.
  • Restrict processing: Temporarily limit how we use your data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
  • Withdraw consent: When processing is based on consent, you may withdraw at any time.

How to Exercise Your Rights

Submit a request to our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at dpo@xnn.xana.gov, including enough information to verify your identity. We will respond within one month, as required by GDPR. You may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your jurisdiction.

Data Transfers Outside the EU/EEA

Our servers and service providers may be located outside the EU/EEA. We rely on standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission, adequacy decisions, or other appropriate safeguards to ensure data protection during international transfers.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in our Privacy Policy or to comply with legal obligations.

Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational measures such as encryption, access controls, and regular audits to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Contact

Questions about GDPR compliance may be directed to our DPO (dpo@xnn.xana.gov) or via our Contact page.
