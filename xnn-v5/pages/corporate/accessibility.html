Accessibility

XNN is committed to providing an inclusive experience for all users. We strive to comply with WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards and continuously improve the accessibility of our website.

Improving Access

  • Text alternatives for non‑text content.
  • Keyboard navigation throughout the site.
  • Proper heading structure and ARIA landmarks.
  • Resizable text and high‑contrast color options.

Feedback

If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact us at accessibility@xnn.com. We welcome your suggestions for improvement.
